Bio

Kerri Chandler is a musician, producer, and DJ from Newark NJ. He has released over 100 records since 1991, and has founded two record labels, Kaoz Theory and Madhouse Records. A DJ's DJ, prolific both in the studio and live, behind a DJ setup or on the keyboards, Kerri is respected globally as an innovator and legend of the international dance music scene.
